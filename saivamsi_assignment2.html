Step 2 & 3: Heuristic Summary

Evaluation Topic: Sports Reporting and Historical Information Retrieval for Educators.

Top 2 Strengths

  • Aesthetic and Minimalist Design: The sports section utilizes high-quality action photography that allows teachers and students to immediately identify the sport before reading the text.
  • Consistency and Standards: All reports follow a standard news layout, ensuring that regular users know exactly where to find the "Read More" links and team statistics.

Top 2 Weaknesses

  • Efficiency of Use: While the "Cardinal Sports Report" is comprehensive, there is no way to filter for one specific team (like "Softball only"), making historical research time-consuming for teachers.
  • Visibility of System Status: The archives list shows the total number of articles (62 for October) but does not indicate how many of those are sports-related versus general campus news until clicked.

PART 2 - Comparison and Suggestions

My analysis showed that while the site is visually engaging for sports fans, it is difficult for researchers looking for specific historical data. My volunteer noted that identifying the number of sports posts in the archives was impossible without opening every month. My technical analysis confirmed this as a "Visibility of System Status" issue regarding how information types are displayed in the sidebar.

Suggestions for Improvement: I recommend adding sub-category filters (e.g., Baseball, Basketball) within the search results to improve efficiency for those researching specific sports history.
