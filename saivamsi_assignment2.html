Step 2 & 3: Heuristic Summary

Evaluation Topic: Armed Conflict (Campus perspectives on war).

Top 2 Strengths

  • Consistency and Standards: The site follows a standard newspaper layout, making it intuitive for teachers to find the "News" and "Archives" sections.
  • Aesthetic and Minimalist Design: The clean interface prevents distractions, allowing students to focus on the primary source materials.

Top 2 Weaknesses

  • Efficiency of Use: There are no advanced search filters (like searching by specific years), which makes historical research time-consuming for teachers.
  • Visibility of System Status: When a search yields no results, the feedback is generic, which can frustrate users trying to find specific historical topics.

PART 2 - Comparison and Suggestions

Comparing my analysis with the mini-usability test, both showed that while the site is easy to navigate for recent news, it becomes difficult when searching for specific historical events like the Vietnam War. My volunteer struggled with the lack of date filters, confirming my heuristic critique regarding efficiency.

Suggestions for Improvement: Implement an advanced search tool that allows users to filter by "Date Range" and "Topic Tags" to better support historical research.
