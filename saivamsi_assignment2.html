Step 2 & 3: Heuristic Summary

Evaluation Topic: Sports Reporting and Historical Information Retrieval for Educators.

Top 2 Strengths

  • Aesthetic and Minimalist Design: The sports section utilizes high-quality action photography that allows teachers and students to immediately identify the sport (e.g., Softball or Lacrosse) visually before reading the text.
  • Consistency and Standards: The reports follow a standard newspaper layout, ensuring that users know exactly where to find the "Read More" links and team statistics.

Top 2 Weaknesses

  • Efficiency of Use: While the "Cardinal Sports Report" is a great summary, there is no way to filter for one specific team within the search results, making historical research time-consuming for teachers.
  • Visibility of System Status: The archives list shows the total number of articles (62 for October) but does not indicate how many of those are sports-related until the user clicks the month.

PART 2 - Comparison and Suggestions

My analysis showed that while the site is visually engaging for sports fans, it is difficult for researchers looking for specific data. My volunteer noted that identifying the number of sports posts in the archives was impossible without opening every month. My technical analysis confirmed this as a "Visibility of System Status" issue regarding the sidebar information.
