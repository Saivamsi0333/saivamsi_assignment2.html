Step 2 & 3: Heuristic Summary

Evaluation Topic: Sports Reporting and Historical Information Retrieval for Educators.

Top 2 Strengths

  • Aesthetic and Minimalist Design: The sports section utilizes high-quality action photography that allows teachers and students to immediately identify the sport before reading the text.
  • Consistency and Standards: All reports follow a standard news layout, ensuring that regular users know exactly where to find the "Read More" links.

Top 2 Weaknesses

  • Efficiency of Use: There is no way to filter results for one specific team or sport, which makes finding historical data time-consuming for teachers.
  • Visibility of System Status: The archives list shows the total number of articles (62 for October) but does not indicate how many are sports-related until clicked.
